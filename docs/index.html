European Medical Device Ontology (MDR-IVDR).: Description back to ToC

It is intended to formalize and categorize knowledge about the European catalog of high-risk medical devices and in vitro diagnostic devices according to their MDR (EU 2017/745) and IVDR (EU 2017/746) regulatory frameworks. The objective of this ontology is to structure and relate the information coming from the EUDAMED public repository covering the entire regulated part of the life cycle of the devices (Manufacturing, risk classification according to regulations and monitoring of their service (incidents and corrective actions).

S1back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the risk associated with an incident based on the risks of the involved device.

Incident c(?/i) , is about device op(?/i,?/d) , has associated risk op(?/d,?/r) -> is about risk op(?/i,?/r)

S2back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the reporting manufacturer of an incident based on the device manufacturer.

Incident c(?/i) , is about device op(?/i,?/d) , has manufactured by op(?/d,?/m) -> is reported by op(?/i,?/m)

S3back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the reporting country of an incident from the manufacturer's location.

Incident c(?/i) , is reported by op(?/i,?/m) , is based in op(?/m,?/c) -> is reported in op(?/i,?/c)

S4back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the risks associated with a device based on its device type.

Risk c(?/r) , affects device type op(?/r,?/dt) , Device c(?/d) , has device type op(?/d,?/dt) -> has associated risk op(?/d,?/r)
